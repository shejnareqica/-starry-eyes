About Us

Welcome to our astronomy club, Starry Eyes.Our goal was to make something that would unite people all around the world and have something in common.

With the making of our club,it makes us happy to see that thousands of people are connecting through us and enjoying our club.

Starry Eyes is a safe space for people who look at the stars every night and eyes light up when a planet is mentioned.So if you are one of those people and need a new hobby, Sign up for free and enjoy daily news and book recommandations and to meet new people who are of the same interests as you

Even though some people may not share the qualities of learning astrophysics,some people(like us)are just interested in knowing stuff withput actually studying them.And our wish was to provide that information
